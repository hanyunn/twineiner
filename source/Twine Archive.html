The work period ends, and you [[go home.]] And that's it. Just another day, right? Yet these are all examples of good and bad conflict. Evenly-matched teams in gym. People yelling their heads off watching others play battle royale games. Debates about whether or not water is wet(it isn't). Those are all examples of conflict that you see daily, and they're not necessarily bad. We shouldn't strive for a world without conflict. Aside from being impossible, you would have to remove many things from life, like sports, games, debates, et cetera. Conflict doesn't always have to be negative; often the skills that you gain in dealing with conflict and the outcome of conflict is better than avoiding it all together.
